/etc/group: 
	- tap tin van ban chua thong tin ve nhom user tren may.
	- chi root moi co quyen thay doi.
	|group_name|password|group_id|additional_member

Tao nhom: groupadd [Options] group_name
	-g: Dinh nghia nhom voi ma nhom GID

Sua thong tin nhom: groupdmod [Options] group_name
	-g GID: sua ma nhom thanh GID
	-n group_name: sua ten nhom thanh group_name

Xoa nhom: groupdel group_name (Xoa user truoc neu ton tai user trong group can xoa)
